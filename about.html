About

Nomada was borne out of a desire for independent, yet safe, female travel.

As the world becomes increasingly more accessible, the prospect of reaching the most remote and interesting places is no longer an unattainable dream. However, for a solo female traveller, this opportunity is greatly restricted by the need to feel safe and comfortable. By accumulating tips and recommendations from a variety of first hand experiences and verifying popular routes ourselves, we can provide trustworthy yet life-changing travel experiences.
Our main services include: Verified female friendly routes, which includes a rating on: Location which has assessed: Overall safety of the location Crime rates Nightlife atmosphere Female friendly accommodation which has assessed: Accommodation safety Accessibility to female only dorms and/or private rooms Female staff available Location (does the accommodation require a walk along secluded streets or is it central?) Demographic of guests that normally choose such accommodation Transport which has assessed: Which transport is most safe for female travellers The routes available Pick-up and drop off locations (i.e. will you be dropped at somewhere remote?) Timing of departures and arrivals (i.e. will you arrive late at night?) Travel experiences which has assessed: Accessibility to staff on the experience (with a higher rating for female staff) Demographic of clientele Whether the experience takes you to remote locations Recommendations and tips for staying safe for each verified route Forum for female travellers to discuss their own experiences and to share their tips and recommendations
